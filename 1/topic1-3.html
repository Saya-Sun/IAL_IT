Technological Convergence

What is Technological Convergence?

Technological convergence is when two or more technologies are combined into a single device, product, or service.

It allows devices to perform multiple functions, making them more useful and convenient in everyday life.

💡 Exam Tip: Always define + give example + explain combined technologies

🌐 Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of devices connected through the internet.

  • Mobile phones, PCs, smart devices
  • Smart fridge, washing machine, heating systems
  • Devices communicate and share data
IoT is a result of technological convergence
📱 Examples of Technological Convergence
1

📺 Smart TV

TV + Internet + Communication
  • Traditional television + web browser
  • Streaming services (movies, shows)
  • Built-in webcam for video calls
2

📱 Smartphone

Phone + Camera + Computer
  • Mobile phone + digital camera
  • GPS navigation + internet access
  • Apps, games, and communication tools
3

⌚ Smartwatch

Watch + Fitness Tracker + Phone Features
  • Tells time + tracks health data
  • Receives messages and notifications
  • Connects to smartphone apps
