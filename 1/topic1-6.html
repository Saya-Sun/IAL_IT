📘 IGCSE IAL IT Topic 1

📊 Device Performance

Performance describes how well a device operates and completes tasks.

Measured using key performance factors
💡 Exam Tip: Always link performance to user needs and efficiency
⚙️ Performance Factors
1

⚡ Speed

Processing instructions quickly

Speed shows how fast a device processes instructions.

  • Measured in GHz (e.g. 2.4 GHz = 2.4 billion instructions/sec)
  • Higher GHz = faster performance
  • More cores = multitasking capability
2

💾 Capacity

Storage size

Refers to how much data a device can store.

  • More storage = more files/apps
  • Improves user convenience
3

🎒 Portability

Ease of carrying

Small and lightweight devices are easier to carry.

  • Important for laptops and phones
  • Not important for devices like TVs
4

🌐 Bandwidth

Data transfer speed

Measures how much data can be sent per second.

  • Higher bandwidth = faster data transfer
  • Improves streaming, downloads, and communication
5

🔋 Power Efficiency

Energy usage

Devices that use less power are more efficient.

  • Longer battery life
  • Lower electricity cost
  • Environmentally friendly
