🌐 Online Working Environments

📌 What is an Online Working Environment?

An online working environment is a virtual workspace where people can work together, share resources and communicate without being physically in the same location.

💡 Exam Tip: Virtual + collaboration + remote working

💼 Examples

  • Adobe Connect
  • Google Workspace
  • Microsoft SharePoint
  • Workplace from Meta
✅ Advantages
1

🏠 Remote Working

Work from anywhere

Users can work from home or office (hybrid working), reducing travel and increasing comfort.

2

💰 Reduced Costs

Lower business expenses

Less need for office space, electricity and maintenance costs.

3

🤝 Collaboration

Work together easily

Users can edit shared documents in real time, avoiding multiple versions.

4

📹 Communication

Easy meetings and training

Video conferencing and screen sharing tools improve communication.

⚠️ Disadvantages
1

🔓 Security Risks

Less secure home networks

Weak security on personal devices can affect the whole system.

2

📉 Data Protection Issues

Risk of data interception

Data sent over the internet may be intercepted without proper encryption.

🧠 Quick Memory

  • Online = virtual office
  • + Remote working
  • + Collaboration
  • – Security risks
  • – Data protection issues
