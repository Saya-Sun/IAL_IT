🌐 Online Communities

📌 What is an Online Community?

An online community is a virtual space where people communicate, interact and share ideas using the internet.

Members usually have a shared interest, such as hobbies, entertainment or professional topics.

💡 Exam Tip: Online + communication + shared interest

📱 Common Platforms

  • Facebook – share life updates with friends/family
  • Instagram – share photos and daily moments
  • Reddit – discussions across many communities
  • TikTok – short videos for entertainment/information
  • Pinterest – ideas and inspiration boards
  • WeChat – messaging and communication
🤔 Why People Join
1

🤝 Belonging

Feel connected

People enjoy being part of a group and may feel less lonely.

2

🎯 Shared Purpose

Common interests

Users interact with others who enjoy the same topics or activities.

3

📚 Increasing Knowledge

Learn and share information

Members can gain knowledge and also teach others.

4

🌍 Global Connection

Connect worldwide

People can communicate with others from different countries.

🧠 Quick Memory

  • Online community = shared interest
  • + Belonging
  • + Knowledge sharing
  • + Global communication
