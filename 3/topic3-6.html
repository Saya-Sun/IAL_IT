💰 Monetising Online Communities

📌 What is Monetisation?

Monetisation is the process of generating income from an online community by using users, content or data.

1

🎯 Targeted Advertising

Ads based on user interests

Advertisements are shown based on users' behaviour, interests or activity within the community.

Example: Gaming community → ads for new games

Benefit: More effective ads → higher revenue

2

🖱️ Pay-per-click (PPC)

Money earned per click

The platform earns a fee every time a user clicks on an advertisement.

Key Point: More clicks = more income

3

📊 Selling User Data

User data sold to companies

Platforms may collect user data (e.g. interests, behaviour) and sell it to companies for marketing purposes.

Risk: Privacy concerns ❗

4

🔒 Paid Subscriptions

Premium content behind paywall

Users pay a subscription to access full features, exclusive content or special services.

Example: Premium videos, member-only areas

5

🤝 Sponsored Content

Companies pay for promotion

Businesses pay to have their products or services promoted within the community.

Example: Sponsored posts, podcasts

⚖️ Evaluation

  • ✔ Generates income for platform owners
  • ✔ Supports free services for users
  • ❗ Can reduce privacy
  • ❗ Too many ads may annoy users

🧠 Quick Memory

  • Ads → targeted
  • PPC → per click
  • Data → sold
  • Subscription → paywall
  • Sponsor → promotion
