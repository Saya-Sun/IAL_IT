🛠️ Designing & Evaluating IT Systems

Evaluation

Checking if system meets requirements

Evaluation determines whether a system is fit for purpose.

💡 Exam Tip: Fit for purpose = meets all requirements

❓ Evaluation Questions

  • What digital devices are used?
  • What peripherals are included?
  • What storage devices are used?
  • What processes are carried out?
  • Does it meet the requirements?
  • Advantages and disadvantages?

🍽️ Example: Restaurant System

📱 Devices & Hardware

  • Customer mobile phone
  • Kitchen computer
  • Printer

💾 Storage

  • 1 TB hard disk drive
  • Database for orders

⚙️ Processes

  • Scan QR code
  • Select menu items
  • Send order to kitchen
  • Print order

✅ Advantages

  • Easy ordering from table
  • Fast communication to kitchen

⚠️ Disadvantages

  • Depends on internet connection
  • Printed orders can be lost

🎯 Fit for Purpose

The system mostly meets requirements but may not fully satisfy concerns about internet dependency.

💡 Exam Tip: Always compare system vs requirements

🧠 Summary

Designing uses diagrams, while evaluation checks if the system meets requirements and is fit for purpose.
