🌍 Environmental Impact of Technology

📌 Overview

Technology affects the environment

The creation, use, and disposal of technology can have both positive and negative impacts on the environment.

1

⚠️ Disrupting Nature

Building factories can destroy natural habitats and affect wildlife ecosystems.

2

⛏️ Consuming Resources

Devices use precious metals like gold and copper, which are limited and can harm the environment during extraction.

3

🏭 Pollution

Manufacturing releases greenhouse gases, water pollution, and noise pollution.

4

🗑️ Waste

Electronic waste and plastic materials can end up in landfill and release toxic chemicals.

💡 E-waste is a major global issue
5

📱 Use of Technology

Millions of people use devices daily, increasing energy consumption and environmental pressure.

6

♻️ Disposal of Technology

Improper disposal leads to pollution and health risks due to toxic materials.

💡 Recycling reduces environmental damage

🧠 Summary

  • Creation → resource use + pollution
  • Use → energy consumption
  • Disposal → e-waste problems
