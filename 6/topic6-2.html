🌱Positive Environmental Impact of Using Technology
1

✅ Monitoring Dangerous Environments

Technology can monitor hazardous environments and automatically trigger safety systems, such as alarms or shutting down machines.

2

🏠 Smart Homes

Smart meters and systems monitor energy usage and reduce waste of electricity and resources.

3

🏙️ Smart Cities

Technology improves transport systems, reduces traffic, and lowers carbon emissions.

4

🌞 Renewable Energy

Technologies like solar, wind, and hydro reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

5

💻 Reduced Resource Use

Digital communication reduces paper, plastic, and fuel use (emails, streaming, online meetings).

💡 Less travel = fewer carbon emissions
⚠️ Negative Environmental Impact of Using Technology
1

⚠️ Energy Consumption

Technology requires electricity, often generated from fossil fuels.

2

🏭 Carbon Emissions

Technologies used in transport and industry increase greenhouse gases.

3

⛏️ Resource Use

Developing new technology uses limited materials like precious metals.

4

🗑️ E-Waste

Frequent upgrades and planned obsolescence lead to electronic waste that pollutes the environment.

💡 E-waste = toxic techno trash

🧠 Summary

  • Positive → efficiency & sustainability
  • Negative → energy use & waste
