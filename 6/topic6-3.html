⚖️ Legal Issues in IT

📌 Overview

Data must be used legally and securely

IT systems process large amounts of data, so laws are needed to protect individuals and organisations.

1

🔐 Data Protection

Laws protect personal data and control how it is collected, stored, and used.

  • Protects individual rights
  • Ensures data is secure
  • Only necessary data is collected
  • Controls who can access data
  • Keeps data up to date
💡 Data stored in other countries may have weaker protection laws
2

©️ Copyright

Protects creators of digital content and gives them rights over their work.

Examples: music, videos, software, websites

💡 You must get permission to copy or use someone else's work
3

💡 Intellectual Property

Creations owned by individuals or companies
  • Patent → protects inventions
  • Trademark → protects brand names/logos
  • Trade secret → protects confidential information
4

🚫 Computer Misuse

Laws prevent illegal use of computers and data.

  • Unauthorised access (hacking)
  • Using data for fraud or identity theft
  • Intercepting data
  • Altering or damaging data
💡 Proving intent is often required in legal cases

🧠 Summary

  • Data protection → protects personal data
  • Copyright → protects creative work
  • IP → protects ideas and inventions
  • Computer misuse → prevents cybercrime
