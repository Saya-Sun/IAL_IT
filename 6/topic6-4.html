⚖️ Moral & Ethical Issues in IT

📌 What are Moral & Ethical Issues?

Right vs wrong use of technology

Ethics and morals are beliefs about what is right and wrong when using IT systems.

1

🔐 Privacy

Technologies like tracking systems and targeted ads collect user data, sometimes without consent.

💡 Privacy vs security is a common debate
2

🤝 Inclusion & Association

Online communities can include or exclude people. AI systems may show bias (e.g. sexism, racism).

3

⚖️ Civil Liberties

Governments may restrict internet content. Some see this as protection, others as loss of freedom.

4

🌍 Access (Digital Divide)

Not everyone has equal access to technology or internet services.

💡 Digital divide = unequal access to technology
5

♿ Accessibility

Systems should include features for users with disabilities.

  • Text-to-speech
  • Speech recognition
  • Screen magnifier
  • Keyboard navigation
6

🗣️ Expression

People want freedom of speech online, but harmful content raises ethical concerns.

💡 Balance between freedom and safety

🧠 Summary

  • Privacy → data tracking concerns
  • Access → digital divide
  • Accessibility → equal use
  • Expression → freedom vs control
