📡 Impact of Wireless Access

📌 Overview

Wireless access is global

Wi-Fi is widely available in homes, cities, and public places, increasing access to information and services.

📍 Location Awareness

Devices can track and share a user’s location using wireless technology.

✅ Positive Impacts

  • Helps emergency rescue (disasters)
  • Improves navigation (GPS)
  • Supports smart city systems

⚠️ Privacy & Safety Risks

  • Users can be constantly tracked
  • Privacy may be invaded
  • Risk of being tracked by criminals
💡 Location data = sensitive personal data

🏢 Business Control

Companies can restrict devices to certain locations for security purposes.

🏙️ Smart Cities

Powered by wireless networks
  • Better transport systems
  • Improved lighting and utilities
  • Reduced traffic and crime

🧠 Summary

  • Wireless access increases connectivity
  • Improves safety and efficiency
  • Raises privacy and security issues
